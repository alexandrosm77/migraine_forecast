About Weather Data Comparison

This feature allows you to compare forecasted weather data with actual recorded weather conditions over time. This helps:

  • Evaluate the accuracy of weather forecasts for your locations
  • Understand how forecast accuracy affects migraine predictions
  • Identify patterns between weather changes and migraine occurrences

The system automatically collects actual weather data and compares it with previous forecasts to generate these reports.
