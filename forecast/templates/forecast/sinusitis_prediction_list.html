About Sinusitis Predictions

Our prediction algorithm analyzes several weather parameters known to trigger sinusitis:

  • Rapid Temperature Changes: Sudden temperature fluctuations (especially cold fronts) can irritate sinuses
  • High Humidity: Promotes mold growth and allergen proliferation
  • Low Humidity: Dries out nasal passages and sinuses, reducing natural defenses
  • Barometric Pressure Changes: Affects sinus pressure and can cause inflammation
  • Precipitation: Can increase airborne allergens and mold spores
  • Cloud Cover: Associated with weather pattern changes

Predictions are classified as:

  • High - Strong likelihood of sinusitis triggers present
  • Medium - Some sinusitis triggers present
  • Low - Few or no sinusitis triggers detected
Note: Sinusitis predictions focus on weather-related triggers. Individual sensitivity varies, and these predictions should not replace medical advice from your healthcare provider.
